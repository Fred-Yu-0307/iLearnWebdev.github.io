The purpose of iLearn WebDev website is to provide a roadmap about back-end and front-end development and supplement the learning materials. Through the website the users are able to monitor their progress that helps the user to become proficient. To avoid users being uninspired the website has a feature that helps it become more interactive to the users.


The goal would be to provide users with a comprehensive and accessible resource that empowers them to achieve their learning goals efficiently and effectively. To achieve this, the website would focus on ensuring that the roadmap is clear, concise, and up to date with the latest developments in the field. It would also strive to make the roadmap interactive and engaging, providing users with opportunities to test their knowledge, track their progress, and receive feedback and guidance. Specifically, the objectives would include; • Defining the core concepts and foundational knowledge required to understand front-end and back-end development. Identifying the essential technologies, tools, and frameworks used in front-end and back-end development and explaining their purpose and use. Providing practical exercises, projects, and assignments that enable learners to apply their knowledge and develop their skills in real-world contexts. Offering guidance and support to Users throughout their learning journey, through feedback. Ensuring that the roadmap is up-to-date and relevant to the latest developments in front-end and back-end development, to equip learners with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving field.

The logo for this website features a fox, a powerful symbol of independence and speed in many cultures. The fox is renowned for its resourcefulness and cleverness, traits that are highly valued in today's fast-paced world. The use of a fox in the logo reflects the idea that users of this website can learn independently and quickly using the about-resources provided.


The fox is a creature that is able to adapt to changing circumstances, which is a valuable trait for anyone who wants to succeed in life. By incorporating the image of a fox into the logo, this website is sending a message to its users that they too can be adaptable, resourceful, and clever. The fox is also known for being a quick learner, which makes it an ideal symbol for a site that is designed to help users learn new things quickly and efficiently.
